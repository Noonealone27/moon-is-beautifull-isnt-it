Priya, you are my Cardio, Shemdi, and Priya Paratha—my heater.

What Does This Mean?

Saying *"The moon is beautiful, isn't it?"* is a poetic way of expressing love. This phrase originates from Japanese culture, where the beauty of the moon reflects quiet, profound emotions of love. Just as the moon’s beauty is timeless and serene, my feelings for you are radiant and eternal. It’s my way of saying, *"I love you."*
